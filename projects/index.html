Projects

Girl Develop It Pittsburgh

I am the chapter leader for the Pittsburgh chapter of Girl Develop It. I help teach and TA classes throughout the year.

Tech speaker support group

I run a semi-regular support group for people interested in getting help and ideas to speak at tech events and conferences. The group focuses on underrepresented speakers, but includes people with a variety of backgroungs and level of experience.

Battling the Invisible Monsters impostor syndrome support group

As a follow-up to my talk about impostor syndrome, I created an online support group for people to discuss impostor syndrome.

Programming interview coaching

I occasionally help people prepare for technical interviews with interview coaching. I focus these efforts on underrepresented groups.

Diversity in tech education

I educate people about how to improve diversity in tech in a variety of ways including writing, public speaking, and discussion.
